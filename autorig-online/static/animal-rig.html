Animal rigging FAQ

Short answers for creators comparing humanoid auto-rigging with animal and creature rigging.

What is AutoRig V2 Animal RIG?

It is the AutoRig.online V2 workflow for animal and non-humanoid models such as quadrupeds, creatures and stylized robot models.

Does it only work for animals?

No. The examples include a spider robot because the same page targets broader non-humanoid rigging problems.

Can I use it without Blender?

Yes. The online version runs through AutoRig.online. A separate Blender plugin exists for users who want a Blender-native workflow.

Which examples are shown here?

The page includes beaver before/after V2 rigging plus animation examples for cat, alpaca, rabbit, turtle and spider robot models.
