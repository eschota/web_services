Why T-Pose Rigging Matters

The T-pose (standing position with arms extended horizontally) is the industry standard for character rigging and animation. It provides the optimal pose for:

Animation Benefits:

  • Retargeting: Easy animation transfer between characters
  • IK Setup: Perfect for inverse kinematics
  • Mocap Data: Compatible with motion capture systems
  • Game Engines: Optimized for Unity and Unreal workflows
