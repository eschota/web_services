Auto Rig FAQ — Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about automatic 3D character rigging, supported formats, pricing, and our service quality.

What 3D file formats do you support for auto rigging?

We support GLB (glTF Binary), FBX, and OBJ file formats. GLB is optimized for web use, FBX works great with Unity and Unreal Engine, and OBJ is perfect for sculpted models and static meshes.

How long does the auto rigging process take?

Most rigging jobs complete within 5-15 minutes, depending on model complexity and current server load. Simple characters process faster, while detailed models with many polygons may take longer.

Is the service really free?

Yes! We offer 3 free rigging conversions per IP address. After that, you'll need to create a free account or purchase credits. All rigging includes professional bone structure and 50+ animations.

What animations are included with rigged characters?

Every rigged character comes with 50+ built-in animations including walking, running, jumping, idle poses, combat moves, and more. All animations are professionally created and optimized for game engines.

Are the rigs compatible with Unity and Unreal Engine?

Yes! Our FBX rigs are fully compatible with Unity's Mecanim system and Unreal Engine's Animation Blueprints. GLB files work great in web applications and modern game engines.

What's the quality of the auto-generated rigs?

Our AI-powered rigging system creates professional-quality bone structures that rival manual rigging from experienced animators. The rigs include proper IK setup, T-pose configuration, and are optimized for performance.

Can I use the rigged characters commercially?

Yes, you can use the rigged characters commercially. However, you should ensure you have the rights to the original 3D models. Our rigging service only adds the animation skeleton - we don't claim ownership of your original models.

What if I'm not satisfied with the rigging result?

If you're not satisfied with the auto-generated rig, you can request a refund within 24 hours. We also offer manual adjustments for premium customers. Contact our support team for assistance.

Do you support custom animations or rigging adjustments?

Our standard service includes 50+ pre-built animations. For custom animations or rigging adjustments, we offer premium services. Contact us for pricing and requirements.

What are the file size limits?

We support models up to 100MB in size. For larger models, please contact us for custom processing options. We recommend optimizing your models before upload for best results.

Can I upload models with multiple materials or textures?

Yes! Our service preserves all materials, textures, UV mappings, and PBR properties. Complex materials with multiple texture maps are fully supported and maintained in the rigged output.

Do you offer API access for developers?

Yes, we offer REST API access for developers who need to integrate auto rigging into their workflows. API documentation and access tokens are available for registered users.
