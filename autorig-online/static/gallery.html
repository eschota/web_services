What is Auto Rig Gallery?

This gallery showcases real results from our AI-powered auto rigging service. Each example demonstrates how static 3D models are transformed into fully animated, game-ready characters. Whether you're working with GLB files for web applications, FBX for Unity and Unreal Engine, or OBJ sculptures from ZBrush/Blender, our service handles the complex rigging process automatically.

Supported Formats & Requirements

All examples shown here started as static models in T-pose position. Our AI analyzes the mesh topology, generates professional bone structures, and applies 50+ animations. The process works with:

  • GLB files: Perfect for web, VR, and modern game engines
  • FBX files: Native support for Unity Mecanim and Unreal Animation Blueprints
  • OBJ files: Converts static sculptures into animated characters

T-Pose Requirements

For best results, prepare your character in T-pose (arms extended horizontally). This standard pose ensures optimal IK setup and animation compatibility. Our service handles various model scales and automatically adjusts bone proportions.

Unity & Unreal Engine Ready

Every rigged character includes multiple export formats: keep the original GLB for web use, download FBX for Unity's Mecanim system, or use the Unreal-compatible version. All rigs include proper naming conventions, IK bones, and constraint setups.

Professional Quality Results

Our AI creates bone structures that rival manual rigging from experienced animators. The rigs include smooth skinning weights, proper deformation, and are optimized for real-time performance in games and interactive applications.
